How to Use Offline on Site
  • 1
    Before arriving on site, open Expo Go and load the app while you have good WiFi. The app bundle is saved to your device.
  • 2
    On site with poor or no signal, open Expo Go directly — the app loads from the saved bundle on your phone without needing network.
  • 3
    All photos, annotations and observations save locally the moment you capture them. Nothing is lost if signal drops.
  • 4
    When signal returns, the app automatically uploads everything in the background. Check the Queue screen for status.
